Who is Little Shadow?

Started with
my dad's recipe.

Little Shadow started with my dad's barbecue sauce — the one I grew up loving as his little shadow. Dementia has made sharing meals more challenging, so I created Little Shadow to make good food easier to bring to the table.

Every bottle is made in Portland, Oregon from recognizable ingredients. No preservatives, no major allergens, no mystery.

A portion of every sale supports dementia research.
